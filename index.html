Cardio Monitoring

Cardio monitoring is a process of tracking and recording the health and functioning of your heart. It typically involves measuring vital signs like heart rate, blood pressure, and sometimes other factors like oxygen levels to ensure your heart is working properly. This monitoring can help detect and manage heart-related issues or conditions.

Medical Treatment

Medical treatment refers to the care and interventions provided by healthcare professionals to diagnose, manage, and cure illnesses or health conditions in patients. It often involves medications, surgeries, therapies, and other medical procedures aimed at improving a person's health and well-being.

Emergency Help

In case of emergency, dial 911 (or your local emergency number) for immediate assistance. Provide clear details and stay on the line until help arrives.

First Aid

First aid is the initial assistance or care provided to a person who has been injured or suddenly taken ill. Its primary goals are to preserve life, prevent the condition from worsening, and promote recovery until professional medical help arrives.
